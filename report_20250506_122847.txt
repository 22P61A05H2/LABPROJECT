## Algoveda Event Report

The Vignana Bharathi Institute of Technology successfully hosted Algoveda, a competitive event held at Vignana Bharathi Institute of Technology, Aushapur, Ghatkesar, Hyderabad, Telangana, India. Visual documentation of the event, including imagery, prominently featured the branding “AlgoVeda, VBIT” and “VBIT, AlgoVeda”, confirming the association with the host institution. Participant feedback indicated a positive experience, with one individual noting, “It was very nice competing with my fellow mates and It was very tough level at the last,” suggesting a challenging and engaging competitive environment.
